The Designer

Straight out
the womb.

Fashion runs in Virginia's family for generations — her mother and grandmother were creatives, and she grew up immersed in an artistic family. Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, Virginia has been designing since before she had words for it.

She created her entire wedding wardrobe herself — and has designed for others' most important moments ever since. She has hosted and planned multiple beauty pageants and fashion shows, each featuring her original designs.

Featured in over 60 publications including Harper's Bazaar Vietnam and showcased at New York Fashion Week, Virginia has built a body of work that honors every individual who wears confidence.
