Virginia's Mission

No Family
Left Behind.

As a Financial Educator and advisor with Revolution Financial Management, I help individuals and families understand life insurance, annuities, retirement strategies, wealth-building concepts, financial literacy, and management mindset building so they can make informed decisions for themselves and future generations.

My mission is simple: No Family Left Behind. Whether I'm creating a garment that boosts someone's confidence or helping a family develop a financial strategy for long-term security, I believe everyone deserves access to knowledge, opportunity, and support.

I take pride in serving with professionalism, integrity, compassion, and a sincere desire to help people achieve their personal, financial, and creative goals.

What Virginia Offers

Knowledge is the foundation.

01

Life Insurance

Understanding life insurance as a foundational financial tool — protecting your family and building long-term security. A cornerstone of any wealth-building strategy, life insurance creates a financial safety net that supports your family regardless of what tomorrow brings.

02

Annuities

Structured financial products that provide guaranteed income streams — building predictability into your financial future. Annuities are a powerful tool for those who want stability and peace of mind in retirement, ensuring income doesn't stop when the paycheck does.

03

Retirement Strategies

Planning the retirement you deserve — with strategies tailored to your timeline, income, and goals. Whether you're just starting out or making your final pre-retirement moves, the right plan transforms a distant dream into a lived reality.

04

Wealth-Building Concepts

The principles that allow ordinary families to build extraordinary legacies — accessible, actionable, and real. Virginia teaches the foundational ideas that separate those who accumulate wealth from those who simply earn income.

05

Financial Literacy Education

Breaking down complex financial concepts into clear, empowering knowledge that anyone can act on. From understanding credit to building a financial foundation, Virginia meets every family where they are and brings them further than they thought possible.

06

Management Mindset Building

Developing the mental frameworks that sustain financial discipline, intentionality, and long-term wealth consciousness. The strategies that work long-term are built on mindset first — and Virginia helps you lay that internal foundation before the external one.

Affiliated Organizations

Financial Services Partner

Revolution Financial Management

RFM is a financial services marketing organization offering financial education, retirement planning, and insurance products to individuals and families. As an affiliate of World Financial Group (WFG), they focus on life insurance, debt solutions, and investment products — making quality financial guidance accessible to the middle market.

Virginia Cathey serves as an independent agent affiliated with these organizations.

World Financial Group (WFG)

WFG, a subsidiary of Transamerica, is a financial services company dedicated to helping middle-market families build lasting financial security. WFG provides its agents with access to a wide range of financial products — including life insurance, retirement planning, and wealth-building strategies — ensuring that everyday families have access to the same level of financial planning previously reserved for the wealthy.

Through her affiliation with WFG, Virginia is empowered to offer a comprehensive suite of solutions tailored to the real needs of the families she serves — no family too small, no goal too large.
