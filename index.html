Her Story
"When they ask how long I've been doing this — I say straight out the womb."

— Virginia Cathey

Virginia Cathey is a Brooklyn-born fashion designer, entrepreneur, and creative visionary with over 40 years of sewing, tailoring, and fashion expertise. Known for her signature hooded bodysuits, reversible garments, and wearable couture designs, Virginia has been featured in more than 60 publications — including Harper's Bazaar Vietnam and Style & Soul Magazine — and has showcased her collections during New York Fashion Week.

As her own seamstress, tailor, stylist, and promoter, she creates versatile designs ranging from ready-to-wear and business attire to red carpet, bridal, eveningwear, and custom couture for clients of all sizes, backgrounds, and expressions.

Beyond fashion, Virginia brings over 20 years of executive-level operations and event management experience, more than 10 years developing sought-after shea butter wellness products, and years in financial education. Her mission: to inspire confidence, provide value, and leave a lasting impression through excellence and authenticity.
