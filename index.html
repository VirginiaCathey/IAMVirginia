Her Story
"When they ask how long I've been doing this — I say straight out the womb."

Brooklyn-born. Generationally gifted. Endlessly creative.

Virginia Cathey, founder of Virginia Cathey Collections, is a Brooklyn-born fashion designer, entrepreneur, and creative visionary with over 40 years of sewing, tailoring, and fashion expertise. Known for her signature hooded bodysuits, reversible garments, and wearable couture designs, Virginia has been featured in more than 60 publications, including Harper's Bazaar Vietnam and Style & Soul Magazine, and has showcased her collections during New York Fashion Week.

As her own seamstress, tailor, stylist, and promoter, she creates versatile designs ranging from ready-to-wear and business attire to red carpet, bridal, eveningwear, and custom couture for clients of all sizes, backgrounds, and expressions. Her work blends elegance, confidence, functionality, and individuality, making luxury fashion feel both empowering and accessible.

Beyond fashion, Virginia brings over 20 years of executive-level operations, event management, and leadership experience within high-performance corporate environments. Combined with more than 10 years developing sought-after shea butter wellness products and several years in financial education, Virginia's diverse background reflects her passion for creativity, empowerment, organization, and helping others thrive.

Whether in business, fashion, finance, or wellness, her mission remains the same: to inspire confidence, provide value, and leave a lasting impression through excellence and authenticity.
